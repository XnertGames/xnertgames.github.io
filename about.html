About Xnert Games

Passionate developers creating innovative tools that empower the game development community

Our Story

Founded in 2021, Xnert Games emerged from a simple observation: game developers needed better tools to create rich, interactive experiences without getting bogged down in technical complexity.

Our journey began when our founders, experienced game developers themselves, repeatedly encountered the same challenges across different projects - cumbersome dialogue systems, poor localization support, and performance bottlenecks that hindered creativity.

Today, we're proud to offer Dialogix, our flagship dialogue system, NPCBrain, our flagship NPC behavior configuration system, and are actively developing additional tools that address real-world development challenges with elegant, performance-focused solutions.
